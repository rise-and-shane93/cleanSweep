Sweeping Compound

Make cleanup fast, easy, and safe!

Our sweeping compound is used to combat everyday floor dirt. When sprinkled onto floors, Clean Sweep instantly attracts dust and dirt thereby creating a cleaner and healthier working environment. In warehouses and stores, Clean Sweep sweeping compound is a moist product that keeps dust and dirt from rising and getting onto items and equipment. In addition, it diminishes inhalation of harmful dust.
