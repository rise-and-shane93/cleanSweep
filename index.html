Thanks for stopping by!

Clean Sweep Products has been manufacturing quality sweeping compounds for over sixty years and serving industrial distributors all across North America. We are considered an industry leader for our superior quality, fast and reliable service, and our low prices. We manufacture compounds for any type of floor. All our compounds are specially formulated with top quality ingredients for maximum dust and dirt control for safe and easy sweeping. In addition, our compounds are brightly colored red or green for spot free sweeping. We offer a wide range of packaging choices, and can also provide private labeling.
